ArchiComedy

Archicomedy is a video content focused marketing platform for comedians

My am a comedian and an entrepreneur with background in marketing. I started doing standup comedy in August 2017 and I've discovered that most comedians love the art but they don't know how to find their audience.

Also, I've noticed that the majority of the comedians who post videos online are the already famous ones who have teams helping them deliver content on platforms such as Facebook and YouTube constantly.

Since I strongly believe that everyone needs to utilise video in the 21st century I am testing my new hypothesis which suggests that people who want to discover new comedians will love to find videos of unknown comedians and that comedians will be able to find new audiences and create a fun base if they utilise video. All while helping comedians to make money from events and more.
